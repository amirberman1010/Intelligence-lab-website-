About the Intelligence Lab
How we investigate construction performance — and why it matters.

The Mission

The Intelligence Lab is dedicated to finding new ways to measure construction performance. Through production benchmarks, industry-specific metrics, and deep data investigation, we shed light on the phenomena that hold projects back — and reveal the opportunities to improve.

The Data

The largest construction performance dataset in existence: activity-level granularity across 10+ verticals and 8+ countries, with both planned and actual outcomes. Impossible to replicate.

Methodology

Min. 4 projects per data point. 25th/50th/75th percentiles. All data aggregated and anonymised. We always show our working.
